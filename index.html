Fight of the Day

Canelo Alvarez vs. Gennadiy Golovkin I

On Sept. 16, 2017, Saul "Canelo" Alvarez and Gennadiy "GGG" Golovkin met in a highly anticipated showdown between two of the best fighters in the world, regardless of weight class.
The fight took place at the T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas, in front of a soldout crowd of 22,358 fans that achieved the third-highest gate in boxing history, surpassing Alvarez's fight with Floyd Mayweather. It also drew 1.3 million domestic PPV buys as the boxing world waited to see a clash between the indestructible force of Golovkin and the boxing brilliance of Alvarez. Golovkin entered the fight as the unified WBA (Super), WBC and IBF champ while Alvarez was recognized as The Ring middleweight title holder.
